Our Story

From sacrifice to soil —
a story of peace

The Peace Orchard is the flagship initiative of Normandy Day UK — a registered charity founded in memory of those who gave their lives in Normandy in 1944. From an act of remembrance came a bold idea: that peace is not just the absence of war, but something we must actively cultivate together.

Located in Coundon Hall Park, Coventry, the orchard is a living landscape — where heritage, nature, education, wellbeing, and community all take root side by side.
