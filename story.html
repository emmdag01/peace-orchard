1944

Normandy, France

Young men left home
so we could live in peace

D-Day, June 1944. Among the thousands who crossed the Channel was Dennis Davison — a soldier who carried hope into the orchards of Normandy.

2011

An act of remembrance

From sacrifice,
a seed of an idea

Dennis Davison BEM founded Normandy Day UK — a registered charity built on the belief that peace isn't passive. It must be cultivated.

2015

Coundon Hall Park · Coventry

They planted
an orchard

Fruit trees took root in Coventry soil — a living monument. Not carved in stone, but breathing. Growing. Open to everyone.

Spring · Summer · Autumn · Winter

And people
came to grow

Families. Schoolchildren. Volunteers. Artists. Neighbours. Season after season, the orchard became something more than trees.

Today

A place to remember.
A space to belong.

Events, workshops, learning, and open green space — for everyone. And the best part? We're just getting started.
